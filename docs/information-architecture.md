# Information Architecture for theaistrugglebus.com

The AI Struggle Bus is a consultancy that helps entrepreneurs, startups and small-to-medium businesses put AI to work behind the scenes. The site needs to feel modern, approachable and credible while highlighting practical outcomes.

---

## Overall Navigation

### Primary Navigation

| Menu Item | Purpose | Notes |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| Home | Introduce who you help, pain points, solutions, outcomes | Acts as narrative overview with CTAs |
| Solutions | Deep dive into AI workflows by function | Bento-style sections with examples |
| Assessment | 10-14 day AI readiness assessment and quiz | Emphasize $50K+ annualized savings |
| 30-Day Value Sprint | Four-week engagement description | Week-by-week activities |
| Results | Outcome stories and case studies | Metrics: hours saved, conversions |
| About | Team experience and credibility | Cross-industry expertise |
| Book a Call (CTA) | Primary CTA button | Opens scheduling form |

### Footer Navigation

Resources, Governance & Safety, Integrations & Data, FAQ, Privacy, Terms

## Page Structure

### 1. Home

**Hero Section**
- Headline: "AI that works for small and medium businesses"
- Sub-text: Focus on embedded AI agents, not feature vendors
- Primary CTA: Book an AI Assessment
- Secondary CTA: Start a 30-Day Value Sprint

**The Pain**
- Manual work friction: copying/pasting, orders, invoices, repetitive questions
- Only 1 in 4 SMBs have fully integrated AI

**What We Do**
- Automate internal workflows
- Agents run inside existing tools (CRM, accounting, communication)
- Embedded workflows vs. "AI features"

**Outcomes**
- Faster launches (weeks to days)
- 4x productivity lift
- $50K+ annualized savings identified

**Solutions Overview**
- Marketing & Sales Automation
- Customer Service & Engagement
- Operations & Finance
- Knowledge & Decision Support
- Product & Development Acceleration

---

### 2. Solutions

Bento grid layout with each solution category:

**Marketing & Sales Automation**
- Product detail pages, campaign kits, customer segmentation
- Benefits: Faster go-to-market, on-brand content, increased conversion

**Customer Service & Engagement**
- AI assistants, complaint summarization, feedback follow-up
- Benefits: 24/7 responsiveness, better satisfaction, actionable insights

**Operations & Finance**
- Report reconciliation, cash-flow forecasting, invoice processing
- Benefits: Timely insights, fewer errors, better cash management

**Knowledge & Decision Support**
- Document ingestion, meeting summarization, competitive intelligence
- Benefits: Faster onboarding, data-driven decisions

**Product & Development Acceleration**
- PRDs, user stories, rapid prototyping
- Benefits: Shortened development cycles, better alignment

---

### 3. Assessment (AI Roadmap & Quiz)

**Process (10-14 days)**
- Days 1-3: Intake and discovery
- Days 4-7: Quantify impact ($50K+ savings)
- Days 8-14: Deliver roadmap

**AI Readiness Quiz**
- 3-minute quiz
- Categories: Early Stage, Workflow Friction, ROI Ready

**Deliverables**
- Opportunity map
- Workflow portfolio
- Savings model
- 30-day sprint plan
- 90-day scale roadmap

---

### 4. 30-Day Value Sprint

**Timeline**
- Week 1: Confirm scope, integrations, KPIs
- Weeks 2-3: Build and embed agents
- Week 4: Measure results, plan next 90 days

**Deliverables**
- 2-5 deployed workflows
- ROI baseline and savings calculation
- Compliance guardrails
- Ownership playbooks
- Scale roadmap

---

### 5. Results

Case study format:
- Situation (challenge)
- Workflows deployed
- Results (metrics)
- Quote/takeaway

---

### 6. About

- Team story and philosophy
- "Built by operators for entrepreneurs"
- Values: Practicality, measurable outcomes, empowerment
- Why different: Embed into existing tools, measure success in hours saved

---

### 7. Resources

- AI Readiness Quiz
- Top 10 AI Mistakes Guide
- Top 10 Red Flags for AI Vendors

---

### 8-11. Secondary Pages

- **Governance & Safety**: Guardrails, compliance, data privacy
- **Integrations & Data**: Supported platforms (Shopify, HubSpot, QuickBooks, etc.)
- **FAQ**: Common questions about the service
- **Privacy/Terms**: Legal pages

## Design Guidelines

See `design-style-guide.md` for complete specifications. Key points:

- Clean neutral base with dark anchor
- Electric blue/teal CTAs, warm coral/yellow accents
- Modern sans-serif typography (Inter, Montserrat, Poppins)
- Bento grid layout
- Scrollytelling with micro-interactions
- Mobile-first, accessible
